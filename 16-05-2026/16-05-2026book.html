My Top 6 Favorite Books

  1. The Midnight Library

    Author: Matt Haig

    Price: $15.99

    A dazzling novel about all the choices that go into a life well lived. Nora Seed finds herself in a library between life and death where she can try out every life she could have lived.

  2. Project Hail Mary

    Author: Andy Weir

    Price: $18.50

    A lone astronaut must save the earth from disaster in this propulsive adventure. It's a tale of survival, self-discovery, and scientific ingenuity.

  3. Dune

    Author: Frank Herbert

    Price: $10.99

    Set on the desert planet Arrakis, this epic science fiction masterpiece tells the story of young Paul Atreides as he accepts stewardship of the most valuable substance in the universe.

  4. The Canterville Ghost

    Author: Oscar Wilde

    Price: $14.00

    The Canterville Ghost is a humorous tale by Oscar Wilde about an American family who moves into a haunted castle, only to find the ghost of a nobleman who killed his wife.

  5. Atomic Habits

    Author: James Clear

    Price: $16.80

    A practical guide to breaking bad habits and building good ones, offering proven frameworks for everyday self-improvement.

  6. The Silent Patient

    Author: Alex Michaelides

    Price: $12.50

    A shocking psychological thriller about a woman's violent act of murdering her husband, and a criminal psychotherapist's obsessive quest to uncover her motive.

Why I Read These Books

  • They explore deep philosophical and psychological questions.
  • They offer a great mix of genres (Sci-Fi, Self-Help, Thriller, Memoir).
  • They inspire new perspectives on everyday life.
